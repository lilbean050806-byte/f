01 About

Crafting
stories
through design

I'm a Global Business and Digital Arts student at the University of Waterloo with a passion for creating meaningful, user-centred experiences. My work sits at the crossroads of strategic thinking and visual creativity.

Beyond the classroom, I founded Sweet Sisters Baking Co. — building a brand from scratch, managing every touchpoint from social media to customer relationships. That hands-on entrepreneurial experience shapes how I approach every design challenge: with both empathy and business acumen.

I bring a multicultural perspective to everything I create, fluent in English, French, and Greek, and deeply committed to design that connects across cultures and communities.
